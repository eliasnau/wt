Once you have created members, you can manage their profiles, update their information, and handle contract changes directly from the dashboard.

## Editing a Member

To update a member's personal or contact information:
1. Locate the member in the list.
2. Click the **Actions** menu (vertical dots) on the right side of the row.
3. Select **Edit**.
4. Update the necessary fields in the form (e.g., address, phone number, notes).
5. Save your changes.

## Managing Groups

You can assign members to specific training groups (e.g., "Kids Class", "Advanced Adults").

1. Open the member's profile (either via Quick View or Full Details).
2. Navigate to the **Groups** section.
3. Use the interface to **Add** or **Remove** the member from groups.
4. Changes are applied immediately.

## Cancelling a Membership

If a member decides to leave your school, you can process their cancellation in the system.

1. Locate the member in the list.
2. Click the **Actions** menu (vertical dots).
3. Select **Cancel Membership**.
4. A dialog will appear showing the **Initial Period End Date**.
5. Confirm the cancellation.

The member will be marked as "Cancelled," and their status will update to reflect the end of their contract period.

<Callout type="warn">
Cancelled members are hidden from the default list view. To see them, ensure you have checked "Include Cancelled Members" in the view options.
</Callout>

## Full Member Profile

For a complete overview, click **View** and then **View Full Details** in the side sheet. The full profile page provides a comprehensive history of the member, including:
- Complete personal data
- Contract history and financial details
- Detailed group assignments
- Notes and logs
