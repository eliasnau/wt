In this tutorial, you will learn how to create a new member in the system.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, ensure you have:
- Admin access to the dashboard

## Instructions

<Steps>
<Step>
 
### Navigate to Members Section

Open your dashboard and click on the **Members** tab in the sidebar navigation.

</Step>
 
<Step>
 
### Click "Add Member"

Locate the "Add Member" button in the top right corner of the member list.


</Step>
 
<Step>
 
### Fill Details

Complete the member form with the following information:

#### Personal Information
- **First & Last Name**: The member's full legal name.
- **Email & Phone**: Contact details for notifications and communication.
- **Guardian Info (Optional)**: If the member is a minor, provide Name, Email, and Phone for a guardian.

#### Address
- **Street, City, State, Postal Code, Country**: The member's billing and residential address.

#### Payment Information
- **IBAN**: The International Bank Account Number for SEPA Direct Debit.
- **BIC**: Bank Identifier Code.
- **Card Holder**: Name of the account holder.

#### Contract Details
- **Start Date**: The month and year the contract begins.
- **Initial Period**: Choose from Monthly, Half Yearly, or Yearly.
- **Fees**: Enter the **Joining Fee** and **Yearly Fee** amounts if applicable.
- **Notes**: Add any specific notes regarding the member or the contract.

Click **Create Member** to finalize the enrollment.

</Step>
 
<Step>
 
### Confirm Creation

Review the details and click **Create**. You should see a success message.

</Step>
</Steps>
