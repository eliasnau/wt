Reduce administrative work by allowing members to sign up or update their profiles themselves using secure access codes.

## Generating Codes

<Steps>
<Step>

### Navigate to Members

Go to **School > Members** in your dashboard.

</Step>
<Step>

### Open QR/Code Generator

Click the **Generate Code** or **QR Code** button in the toolbar.

</Step>
<Step>

### Select Type

Choose the type of access you want to provide:
- **New Member Signup**: Creates a blank form for new students to register.
- **Profile Update**: Generates a unique link for an existing member to update their details (e.g., address, emergency contact).

</Step>
<Step>

### Share

Print the QR code for your front desk or send the link via email/SMS to the member.

</Step>
</Steps>

## How it Works

1. The member scans the QR code or visits the link.
2. They are taken to a secure form on the **Member Portal**.
3. They fill out their information.
4. Upon submission, the data is automatically updated in your system pending your approval.
