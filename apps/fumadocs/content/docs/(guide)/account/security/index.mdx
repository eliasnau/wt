Keeping your account secure is critical, especially when handling member data. We provide several tools to help you protect your account.

![Security Overview](/images/account/security-overview.png)

Manage your security settings at [domain.com/account/security](https://domain.com/account/security).

<Callout type="info">
  If you have any questions or concerns regarding your account security, please do not hesitate to **contact support**.
</Callout>

## Available Security Features

### [Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)](./security/2fa)
Add an extra layer of security by requiring a code from your phone when logging in.

## Password Management

<Callout type="warn">
  **Support will NEVER ask for your password.** If anyone asks for your password, it is a scam.
</Callout>

You can change your password at any time from the main Security page.
1. Enter your current password.
2. Enter and confirm your new strong password.
3. Click **Update Password**.
