## School Profile

Update your school's public-facing details that appear on invoices and emails.

- **School Name**: The name of your dojo/gym.
- **Logo**: Upload your school crest.
- **Contact Info**: Phone, email, and physical address.

## Localization

- **Timezone**: Ensure this is set correctly for class schedules.
- **Currency**: Set your default currency for payments.
