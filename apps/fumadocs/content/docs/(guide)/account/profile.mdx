Your profile contains the personal details associated with your user account. This information is visible to the organizations you are a member of.

## Updating Information

<Steps>
<Step>
 
### Navigate to Profile

Click on your avatar in the sidebar and select **Account Settings**, or navigate directly to [Account > Profile](https://domain.com/account).

</Step>
<Step>
 
### Edit Details

![Profile Settings](/images/account/profile-settings.png)

You can update the following fields:
- **Display Name**: This is how you appear to other members and on schedules.
- **Email Address**: Your primary contact method and login identifier.

</Step>
<Step>
 
### Save Changes

Click **Save** to apply your updates.

</Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting Account

<Callout type="warn">
  Deleting your account is permanent. All your personal data will be removed.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info">
  You cannot delete your account if you are currently a member of any organization. You must leave or delete all organizations first.
</Callout>

To permanently delete your account:
1. Scroll to the bottom of the Profile page.
2. Click **Delete Account**.
3. Type the confirmation phrase when prompted.
4. Confirm the deletion.
