Your user account can belong to multiple organizations (e.g., different martial arts schools or branches). The **Organizations** page lets you manage these connections.

## Switching Organizations

![Organization Switcher](/images/account/org-switcher.png)

If you are a member of multiple organizations, you can switch between them instantly.

1. Click the **Organization Name** at the top of the sidebar.
2. Select the organization you want to access from the dropdown list.

## Leaving an Organization

If you no longer work with a specific school, you can leave the organization.

<Steps>
<Step>
 
### Go to Account Settings

Navigate to [Account > Organizations](https://domain.com/account/organizations).

</Step>
<Step>
 
### Locate Organization

Find the organization you wish to leave in the list.

</Step>
<Step>
 
### Leave

Click the **Leave** button. You will lose access to all data and resources within that specific organization immediately.

</Step>
</Steps>
