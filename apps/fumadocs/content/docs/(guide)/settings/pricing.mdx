## Detailed Features

### Starter
*Good for anyone who is self-employed and just getting started.*
- **Price**: $24/mo (Monthly) or $20/mo (Annual)
- **Limits**: Up to 150 members, 2 Users
- **Support**: Free Support

### Pro
*Perfect for small teams and growing businesses.*
- **Price**: $50/mo (Monthly) or $42/mo (Annual)
- **Limits**: Up to 500 Members, 5 user accounts
- **Features**:
    - Everything in Starter
    - Member Portal
    - Free Priority support

### Enterprise
*For large organizations with specific needs.*
- **Price**: Contact Sales
- **Limits**: Unlimited Members, Unlimited user accounts
- **Features**:
    - Everything in Pro
    - Custom Branding
    - Dedicated support

## Starting Dojo Discount

We offer a special plan designed for new studios just getting started.

<Callout type="info" title="Just starting out?">
  If you have fewer than **25 members**, you can apply for our **Starting Dojo Discount**.
</Callout>

### Offer Details
- **First Month**: Free
- **Next 4 Months**: Discounted rate
- **Eligibility**: Must have fewer than 25 active members.

To apply, visit the [Application Page](https://domain.com/apply-starting-discount).
