---
title: General Settings
description: Manage your organization's profile and settings.
---

Navigate to **Settings > General** to manage your organization's core details.

## Organization Info

Update your organization's public-facing information:
- **Name**: The name of your martial arts school or organization.

## Organization Slug

The slug identifies your organization in URLs (e.g., `app.yourschool.com/org-slug`). changing this will update your links.

## Custom Domain

Configure a custom domain (e.g., `members.yourschool.com`) for your organization to provide a branded experience for your members.
